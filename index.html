In this page, you can download Mathematical-specific thesaurus and English-specific the thesaurus generated by our tool. Except for the thesauruses, you can also find other related datasets. If you have any questions, please contact me with dunnzhang0@gmail.com

File Name Date Description
eng_domain_vocab.json 2020-06-19 All words and their frequencies in English-specific corpus(https://archive.org/details/stackexchange)
eng_domain_terms.txt 2020-06-19 The English-specific domain terms. For example, `simple_past_tense` and `formal_writing`
eng_semantic_related_words.json 2020-06-19 This file includes terms and their semantic-related words (e.g. formal_writing: academic_writing)
eng_final_thesaurus.json 2020-06-19 The final thesaurus, which includes terms and their synonyms and abbreviations.
math_domain_vocab.json 2020-06-19 All words and their frequencies in Mathematical-specific corpus(https://archive.org/details/stackexchange)
math_domain_terms.txt 2020-06-19 The Mathematical-specific domain terms. For example, `decimal_expansion` and `inner_products`
math_semantic_related_words.json 2020-06-19 This file includes terms and their semantic-related words (e.g. decimal_expansion: decimal_expansions)
math_final_thesaurus.json 2020-06-19 The final thesaurus, which includes terms and their synonyms and abbreviations.
